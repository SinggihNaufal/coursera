# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to create a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful
- Use inclusive language
- Accept constructive criticism

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Maintainers are responsible for enforcing standards.

## Scope
Applies to all project spaces.

## Enforcement
Violations will be reviewed and addressed.

## Enforcement Guidelines
- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban
